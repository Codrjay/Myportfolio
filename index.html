Project 01 · Strategy and Consulting
Strategic Analysis Market Research Executive Presentation West Africa

Navigating West Africa's Growth Wave — Alpha Inc. Expansion Strategy

Prepared a board-level strategic report for Alpha Inc. evaluating market expansion across Nigeria, Ghana, and Côte d'Ivoire. The analysis combined GDP trend data, CAGR comparisons, port efficiency scores, AfCFTA readiness ratings, and correlation analysis to determine the optimal expansion market.

Ran correlation analysis across three variable pairs and found a near-perfect inverse relationship (r = -0.98) between economy size and growth rate, confirming a catch-up growth effect. Ghana led on every agility metric despite Nigeria's commanding $490B GDP.

Recommendation delivered: Pivot to Ghana within three years to capitalise on its 4.9% CAGR and low entry friction, defend Nigeria, and monitor Côte d'Ivoire for medium-term expansion.

Project 02 · Investment and Venture Analytics
Global Dataset Power BI R Programming Investor Report

Unicorn Investigation Report — Global Startup Valuation Analysis

An investor-facing investigation into the distribution, growth patterns, and ROI of unicorn companies globally. The analysis covered geography, industry, time-to-unicorn, and investment returns across countries, cities, and individual companies.

Data cleaning required converting text-formatted financial fields ($1B, $1M) into numeric values for arithmetic, and resolving a data anomaly where Yidian Zixun showed a negative time-to-unicorn due to an incorrect founding year. The correct year was independently verified and updated before analysis proceeded.

Key insight: Sunnyvale's ROI of 402,946 dwarfs every other city despite having only 7 unicorns. Zapier achieved a 399,900x return on $1M funding. Fintech and Internet Software lead all industries, and the average journey to unicorn status takes 6 to 10 years, with 2021 as the peak year.

Project 03 · Marketing Analytics
Marketing Analytics Customer Segmentation 3-Part Series

Maximizing Marketing Impact — Offer Engagement Analysis

A three-part analysis of customer offer engagement across income groups, gender, and age, helping a marketing team understand who engages with offers, who actually converts, and why.

Used scatter plots to test four correlations. Found a positive relationship between income and completion rate, and between age and completion rate, but no clear relationship between income or age and viewing rate.

Key insight: Females consistently outperform in offer completion. Middle Income males dominate in transaction volume at 47,000 transactions, making them prime targets for volume campaigns. The "Other" gender segment shows an 89% viewing rate, small but highly engaged and worth a dedicated strategy.

Project 04 · Sales Analytics
Sales Analytics Google Sheets Multi-Table Data

CPM Sales Analysis — Computer Hardware Company

Analysed the sales pipeline of a computer hardware company to answer four business questions: which region is performing best, are any agents falling behind, are there quarter-on-quarter trends, and which products have the best win rates.

Used XLOOKUP to merge multi-table data, inspected and validated blank fields against business logic. Open deals cannot have close values, so blanks here are valid, not dirty data. Built charts to surface findings clearly across all three regional teams.

Key insight: The Western region led in revenue at $3.57M while Central closed the most deals. Top agent Darcel Schlecht generated $1.15M alone. GTX Basic had a high win rate but low revenue, a clear pricing strategy red flag.

Project 05 · Retail Analytics
Retail Analytics Power BI 2015 to 2018 3-Part Series

Uncovering Secrets in Our Business — Retail Store Deep Dive

A three-part retail analysis covering four years of store data. Part 1 focused on losses and low performance. Part 2 examined profit trends. Part 3 looked at sales patterns, together delivering a complete business diagnostic.

Identified $156K in total losses across 1,871 cases. A key nuance uncovered: the least-profitable product and the worst-selling product were different items, a distinction most businesses overlook entirely. Losses concentrated in Central and East regions with a recurring Q4 seasonal spike every year.

Built a full Power BI dashboard for stakeholder self-service analysis, enabling the business to explore findings independently without needing an analyst present.
